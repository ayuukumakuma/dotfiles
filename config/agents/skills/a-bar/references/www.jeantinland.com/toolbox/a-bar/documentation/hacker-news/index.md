# Hacker News

> Published on Mon, Feb 9, 2026> Updated on Mon, Feb 9, 2026

## Description [#](https://www.jeantinland.com/toolbox/a-bar/documentation/hacker-news/index.html#description)

The Hacker News widget displays the top stories from Hacker News with automatic rotation. It uses the Algolia API to fetch the latest stories and updates at a configurable interval. Each story shows its title, points, and a link to the original post.

## Interactions [#](https://www.jeantinland.com/toolbox/a-bar/documentation/hacker-news/index.html#interactions)

* Click to open the current story in your browser.
* Stories rotate automatically based on the rotation interval.
* Click on the arrow button to open a list of the latest 20 stories.
* Right‑click to refresh immediately.

## Available settings [#](https://www.jeantinland.com/toolbox/a-bar/documentation/hacker-news/index.html#available-settings)

* `Show icon`: Show or hide the Hacker News icon. **Default is true**.
* `Show points`: Display story points/score. **Default is true**.
* `Max title length`: Maximum characters for story titles. **Default is 50**.
* `Refresh interval`: Refresh cadence in seconds. **Default is 300**.
* `Rotation interval`: Story rotation cadence in seconds. **Default is 10**.
